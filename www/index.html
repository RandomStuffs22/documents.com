TaskPaper.com

Simple TaskPaper to-do list syncing

For TaskPaper (Mac application) users who want to sync their to-do lists online. TaskPaper.com stores your lists and syncs them between your work, home, and (iPhone soon). Unlike generic sync solutions TaskPaper.com is designed specifically for TaskPaper files.

  • Beta. Things may change and go wrong.
  • Light Security. Not for sensitive information, credit cards, passwords, etc.
  • Hog Bay Software. Creator of TaskPaper and WriteRoom Mac applications.
  • Google App Engine. Scalable and reliable. Sign in with your Google ID.
  • Support. Join the discussion in TaskPaper's user forums.
